Six Ways to Earn with ShippingTrade

The container shipping industry generates revenue across multiple streams — ShippingTrade gives you access to all of them, automatically

📦

Container Leasing

Shipping containers are the backbone of global trade — there are over 50 million in circulation worldwide. Your capital funds containers leased to top carriers, generating steady monthly income per unit.

€300 – €800 / container / month
🚢

Freight Revenue Share

Global container freight generates $350B/year in revenues. ShippingTrade investors receive a proportional share from completed voyages across the world's busiest trade routes.

€500 – €2,200 / voyage share
🛡️

Cargo Insurance Commissions

Every container shipped is insured. Investors earn commission from insurance premiums bundled with carrier shipments — a low-risk, high-volume passive stream from the $50B maritime insurance market.

2% – 5% of insured cargo value
📊

Carrier Equity & Dividends

The world's top shipping carriers generate billions in profit annually. Access equity positions in industry leaders — Maersk (listed), CMA CGM, MSC — with quarterly dividend distributions.

8% – 22% annual dividend yield
⚓

Port Terminal Revenue

Container terminals charge handling fees on every TEU that passes through. Rotterdam processes 15.3M TEUs/year, Singapore 38M. Investors earn micro-fees across this massive volume.

€0.40 – €1.20 per TEU processed
⛽

Bunker Fuel Optimisation

Bunker fuel is the single largest operating cost in shipping (~$200B/year industry-wide). Our platform participates in carrier fuel hedging strategies, distributing optimisation profits to investors.

€200 – €700 / quarter
